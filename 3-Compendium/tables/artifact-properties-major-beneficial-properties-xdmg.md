# Artifact Properties; Major Beneficial Properties
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 224* 

**Major Beneficial Properties**

`dice: [](artifact-properties-major-beneficial-properties-xdmg.md#^major-beneficial-properties)`

| dice: 1d100 | Property |
|-------------|----------|
| 01–20 | While attuned to the Artifact, one of your ability scores (DM's choice) increases by 2, to a maximum of 24. |
| 21–30 | While attuned to the Artifact, you regain `dice:1d6\|noform\|avg` (`1d6`) Hit Points at the start of each of your turns if you have at least 1 Hit Point. |
| 31–40 | When you hit with an attack roll while attuned to the Artifact, the target takes an extra `dice:1d6\|noform\|avg` (`1d6`) Force damage. |
| 41–50 | While you're attuned to the Artifact, your Speed increases by 10 feet. |
| 51–60 | While attuned to the Artifact, you can cast one level 4 spell (chosen by the DM) from it. After you cast the spell, roll `dice:1d6\|noform\|avg` (`1d6`). On a roll of 1–5, you can't cast it again in this way until the next dawn. |
| 61–70 | As 51–60 above, except the spell is level 5. |
| 71–80 | As 51–60 above, except the spell is level 6. |
| 81–90 | As 51–60 above, except the spell is level 7. |
| 91–00 | While attuned to the Artifact, you have Immunity to the [Blinded](3-Compendium/rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](3-Compendium/rules/conditions.md#Deafened), [Petrified](3-Compendium/rules/conditions.md#Petrified), and [Stunned](3-Compendium/rules/conditions.md#Stunned) conditions. |
