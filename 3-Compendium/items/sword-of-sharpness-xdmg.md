# Sword of Sharpness
*Very rare (requires attunement)*  
![](3-Compendium/items/img/sword-of-sharpness.webp#right)


When you attack an object with this magic weapon and hit, maximize your weapon damage dice against the target.

When you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the `dice:d20|noform|avg` (`d20`) for the attack roll, that target takes an extra 14 Slashing damage and gains 1 [Exhaustion](3-Compendium/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level.

**Variants**:
- [Glaive of Sharpness](#Glaive%20of%20Sharpness)
- [Greatsword of Sharpness](#Greatsword%20of%20Sharpness)
- [Longsword of Sharpness](#Longsword%20of%20Sharpness)
- [Scimitar of Sharpness](#Scimitar%20of%20Sharpness)
