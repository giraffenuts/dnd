# Expeditious Retreat
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minute

You take the [Dash](3-Compendium/rules/actions.md#Dash) action, and until the spell ends, you can take that action again as a [Bonus Action](3-Compendium/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md).
