# Ice Storm
*4th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 300 feet
- **Components:** V, S, a mitten
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Hail falls in a 20-foot-radius, 40-foot-high Cylinder centered on a point within range. Each creature in the Cylinder makes a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes `dice:2d10|noform|avg` (`2d10`) Bludgeoning damage and `dice:4d6|noform|avg` (`4d6`) Cold damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

Hailstones turn ground in the Cylinder into [Difficult Terrain](3-Compendium/rules/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md) until the end of your next turn.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The Bludgeoning damage increases by `dice:1d10|noform|avg|text(1d10)` for each spell slot level above 4.
