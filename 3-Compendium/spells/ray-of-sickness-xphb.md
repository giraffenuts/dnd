# Ray of Sickness
*1st-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You shoot a greenish ray at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `dice:2d8|noform|avg` (`2d8`) Poison damage and has the [Poisoned](3-Compendium/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition until the end of your next turn.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `dice:1d8|noform|avg|text(1d8)` for each spell slot level above 1.
