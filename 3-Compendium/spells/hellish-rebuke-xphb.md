# Hellish Rebuke
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 reaction
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

The creature that damaged you is momentarily surrounded by green flames. It makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking `dice:2d10|noform|avg` (`2d10`) Fire damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `dice:1d10|noform|avg|text(1d10)` for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Warlock](list-spells-classes-warlock); [Warlock](list-spells-classes-warlock)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 284. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
