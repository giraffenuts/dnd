# See Invisibility
*2nd-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a pinch of talc
- **Duration:** 1 hour

For the duration, you see creatures and objects that have the [Invisible](3-Compendium/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) condition as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Creatures and objects there appear ghostly.
