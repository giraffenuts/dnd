# Darkvision
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a dried carrot
- **Duration:** 8 hour

For the duration, a willing creature you touch has [Darkvision](3-Compendium/rules/senses.md#Darkvision) with a range of 150 feet.
