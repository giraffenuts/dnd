# Magic Missile
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You create three glowing darts of magical force. Each dart strikes a creature of your choice that you can see within range. A dart deals `dice:1d4+1|noform|avg` (`1d4 + 1`) Force damage to its target. The darts all strike simultaneously, and you can direct them to hit one creature or several.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The spell creates one more dart for each spell slot level above 1.
